How It Works

Our simple three-step process makes it easy to create meaningful impact through your donations

ChariTree is more than just a donation platform—it's a movement to turn generosity into lasting, visible change. Every donation you make is tracked, rewarded, and transformed into real trees planted across communities in need. Here's how you become part of the impact:

Donate

Contribute money, food, clothes, or essentials through our verified charity partners. Every donation is logged and tracked for transparency.

Earn

Receive XP points based on your donation value. XP is your impact score—track it, compete with friends, and unlock rewards as you give.

Plant

Convert your XP into real trees planted across Manila. See your trees on the map, dedicate them, and watch your forest grow!

Why Trees?

  • Environmental Impact: Trees absorb CO₂, clean the air, and help fight climate change.
  • Community Benefits: Tree planting creates jobs, improves local health, and provides food and shelter for communities.
  • Legacy: Every tree you plant is a living legacy, growing for generations to come.

What Makes ChariTree Different?

  • Transparency: Track every donation and see exactly where your impact goes.
  • Gamified Giving: Earn XP, level up, and unlock badges as you help others.
  • Community: Join a network of changemakers and see your collective impact grow.
